Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

GiftSmash respects your privacy. This page explains what information may be collected when you use the app.

GiftSmash does not require you to create an account and does not collect personal information such as your name or email address.

Advertising

The app may display advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Google may collect limited device information to deliver and measure ads in accordance with its own Privacy Policy.

In-App Purchases

GiftSmash offers an optional "Remove Ads" purchase. Payments are processed securely by Apple. The developer does not receive or store your payment information.

Analytics

GiftSmash does not use its own analytics service to identify users.

Children

GiftSmash is not directed toward children under the age required by local laws.

Your Choices

Depending on your region, you may be asked for consent regarding personalized advertising. You can also manage your privacy settings directly on your device.
